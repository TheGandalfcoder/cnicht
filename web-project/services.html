Our Services

Cnicht Cleaning Services takes pride in offering specialised cleaning services for holiday properties. Their dedicated team ensures that holiday homes are immaculate and ready to welcome guests. They understand the importance of making a great impression on visitors, and their cleaning services guarantee a clean and inviting environment.
Their deep cleaning service revitalises spaces. They go beyond the surface, tackling hidden dirt, grime, and germs. Their deep cleaning services guarantee a fresh and hygienic environment, whether a home, office, or commercial property. Experience the difference in cleanliness and enjoy a pristine atmosphere.
Construction and renovation projects often leave a mess behind. Cnicht Cleaning Services' after-build cleaning service is here to restore properties to their pristine state. They remove debris, dust, and residue, leaving spaces ready for use or occupancy.
When it's time to move out, leave the cleaning to them. Their end-of-tenancy cleaning service ensures properties are spotless and in excellent condition for the next occupant. They pay meticulous attention to detail, meeting the highest cleaning standards.
Cnicht Cleaning Services understands the importance of a clean and comfortable home. Their domestic cleaning service is customised to meet the unique needs of households. Whether clients require regular maintenance or occasional cleaning, their team is here to assist. The professional cleaners at Cnicht Cleaning Services bring a wealth of experience and dedication to every home they service.
For holiday property owners, their check and refresh services are essential. They inspect properties, ensuring everything is in working order, and provide a quick refresh to maintain inviting appeal. This service is ideal for keeping holiday homes in top condition between guest stays.
A clean log burner enhances efficiency and contributes to a safer environment. Cnicht Cleaning Services' log burner cleaning service effectively removes creosote and soot build-up, ensuring optimal operation of log burners.
Their key holding service offers peace of mind. They securely hold and manage keys to various properties, ensuring access is available when needed.
