About Cnicht Cleaning Services

Cnicht Cleaning Services is a family-owned and eco-conscious cleaning company specialising in holiday home and deep cleaning services with over 14 years of experience. They are committed to providing top-notch cleaning solutions tailored to your needs.

Based in Snowdonia, North Wales: Nestled in the breathtaking beauty of Snowdonia, North Wales, Cnicht Cleaning Services finds inspiration in its stunning surroundings. They serve the picturesque region of Gwynedd and its surrounding areas.

Bringing Cleaning Excellence to You: Cnicht Cleaning Services offers mobile cleaning services, ensuring convenience, professionalism, and expertise at your doorstep. Whether you need a one-off deep clean or regular maintenance, their team can meet all your cleaning needs.

Unique Selling Points

Friendly and Professional Service

Known for their friendly and professional approach, the team at Cnicht Cleaning Services treats your space as if it were their own, adding a personal touch to every cleaning job.

Fully Insured

Cnicht Cleaning Services is fully insured, safeguarding you and your property. They stay updated on fire safety changeover procedures and maintain digital records for all clients, ensuring compliance. Their staff members undergo fire warden training, contributing to a heightened level of safety.

Ethical and Eco-Friendly

As an ethically run business, they specialise in eco-friendly cleaning services, demonstrating a commitment to preserving the environment.

Company History

Established as a partnership in 2019, Cnicht Cleaning Services has evolved into two separate businesses: Cnicht Cleaning Services and Cnicht External Cleaning Services. This recent development reflects their commitment to providing specialised and tailored cleaning solutions to meet your needs.

Experience That Counts

With cleaning experience dating back to 2010, their team has built a solid foundation of expertise and excellence. Their journey began with a vision to offer top-quality, ethical, and professional cleaning services to the local Gwynedd community and beyond.

Trusted by Leading Names

Their commitment to excellence has earned them the trust of some of the most respected organisations in the industry.
